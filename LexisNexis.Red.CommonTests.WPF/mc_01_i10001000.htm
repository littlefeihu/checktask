DIVISION I A Note on the Format 

Malaysian Conveyancing will deal with legislation and matters of concern to conveyancing in both Peninsular Malaysia and East Malaysia. It is intended for each Division from Division II to Division X to be divided into two parts, Part One dealing with Peninsular Malaysia and Part Two dealing with East Malaysia.

The format of the text is as follows.

Division II

Division II contains several headings under which the conveyancing transaction in Peninsular Malaysia is discussed. These include:

  • (1) the relationship between solicitor and client;
  • (2) the contract between vendor and purchaser;
  • (3) checklists of many of the matters which should be considered by a prudent conveyancer;
  • (4) the preparation and exchange of the contract, caveating, completion, and post-completion; and
  • (5) the liability of the solicitor towards the client and third parties.

Division III

Division III deals with conveyancing practice and procedures, and the appropriate precedents relating to it. It is intended to set out in detail the practical side of conveyancing practice with effective precedents used in actual practice, including precedents for transactions that involve foreigners, with relevant provisions to deal with the necessary application for the relevant approvals for foreigners.

Division IV

Extracts from legislation relevant to conveyancing are recited in Division IV Part One, including Federal, as well as some state legislation. These include the Housing Development (Control and Licensing) Act 1966 (Act 118) and the Regulations made thereunder, the Contracts Act 1950 (Act 136), the Padi Cultivators (Control of Rent and Security of Tenure) Act 1967 (Act 528), the Companies Act 1965 (Act 125), the Specific Relief Act 1950 (Act 137) and the Kelantan Land Settlement Act 1955 (Act 460) (which was first enacted as the Kelantan Land Settlement Ordinance 1955 (No51 of 1955)). Outline notes on each legislation are also included.

In Part Two, legislation relevant to conveyancing in Sabah and Sarawak are extracted and inserted thereto. Outline notes on the legislation will also be included.

Division V

Division V contains the Forms and Schedules to the National Land Code (Act 56 of 1965).

Division VI

Division VI contains detailed commentaries on matters relevant to the conveyancing transaction, including the formation of contract, the fiduciary role of the solicitor and the forms of security transactions.

Division VII

Division VII provides extracts from conveyancing decisions with commentaries on the topics raised therein. Many of these matters are discussed further in Division VI.

Division VIII

Division VIII discusses co-ownership.

Division IX

Division IX will contain various Practice Directions, including Circulars from the court and land registries.

Division X

In Division X, extracts of relevant, miscellaneous legislation will be set out with explanatory notes. It is intended to include in this Division relevant sections from the National Land Code pertaining to the conveyancing practice.

Division XI

Division XI will contain extracts of State Land Rules.

As the book is published in loose-leaf form, it will be easier and quicker to update the materials contained herein.
